Refund Policy

Last updated: August 16, 2025

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our courses. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

• Request must be made within 30 days of purchase
• Applies to all course purchases
• No questions asked policy
• Full purchase amount refunded

2. How to Request a Refund

Email us at: algerebakc@allenralfordbarkley.com

Include:
• Your name and email address used for purchase
• Course name and purchase date
• Brief reason for refund (optional)

3. Refund Processing

• Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
• Refunded to original payment method
• Course access will be revoked upon refund
• Email confirmation sent when processed

4. What Happens After Refund

• Immediate loss of course access
• All downloaded materials must be deleted
• Account remains active for future purchases
• You may repurchase the course at any time

5. Exceptions

• Refunds not available after 30-day period
• Promotional or discounted courses follow same policy
• Consulting services have separate refund terms

6. Payment Processor

Refunds are processed through Paddle, our secure payment processor. Processing times may vary based on your bank or payment method.

7. Contact Information

Questions about refunds or need assistance?
Email: algerebakc@allenralfordbarkley.com
Response Time: Within 24 hours

8. Fair Use Policy

We trust our customers and offer generous refund terms. We reserve the right to limit refunds for accounts that abuse this policy.
